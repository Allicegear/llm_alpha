This dataset provides comprehensive coverage of corporate and macroeconomic events, including earnings releases, conference calls, IPOs, SEC filings, and economic indicators. It includes detailed event metadata, verbatim transcripts, analyst briefs, and supporting materials such as presentations and audio recordings. The data is structured with identifiers like RIC, CUSIP, SEDOL, and ISIN, and is updated frequently with both current and historical records. By tracking the timing, content, and sentiment of key corporate events and macroeconomic announcements, this dataset enables investors and analysts to anticipate market-moving news, assess management tone and guidance, and identify catalysts that may impact stock prices and market volatility.
