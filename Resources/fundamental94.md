This dataset provides comprehensive, standardized financial statement data for public companies worldwide, covering over 96% of global market capitalization. It includes annual and interim/quarterly balance sheets, income statements, cash flow statements, per share data, calculated financial ratios, pricing, and company descriptive information. The data is mapped to multiple security identifiers (e.g., ISIN, CUSIP, SEDOL, ticker) and is available in both point-in-time and restated formats, ensuring historical accuracy and comparability. With over 1,500 data items, the dataset enables in-depth analysis of company fundamentals, peer benchmarking, and the construction of predictive models for stock price movements. Its breadth and consistency make it a valuable resource for quantitative research, backtesting, and the development of alpha-generating investment strategies.
