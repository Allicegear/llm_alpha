This dataset provides a comprehensive mapping between Reuters Instrument Codes (RIC) and Bloomberg identifiers for financial securities. It is essential for cross-referencing and integrating data from different financial data providers, enabling analysts and systems to unify disparate datasets for more robust analysis. While it does not directly contain price or fundamental data, its utility lies in facilitating accurate data merging, which is crucial for building reliable models and conducting multi-source research that can ultimately improve price movement predictions.
